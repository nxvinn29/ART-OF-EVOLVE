# Product Roadmap

This document outlines the high-level roadmap for the Art of Evolve application. It is a living document and subject to change based on user feedback and technical discovery.

## Q1 2026: Foundation & Stability (Current)

- [x] **Core Features**: Habit tracking, Journaling, Goals.
- [x] **Testing**: comprehensive unit and widget test coverage (Target: 80%).
- [x] **CI/CD**: Automated build and test pipelines.
- [ ] **Documentation**: Complete API and architecture docs.
- [ ] **Localization**: Initial support for Spanish and French.

## Q2 2026: Social & Community

- [ ] **Social Sharing**: Share achievements and streaks on social media.
- [ ] **Community Challenges**: Global challenges for users to join.
- [ ] **Friends System**: Add friends and view their progress.
- [ ] **Leaderboards**: Weekly and monthly leaderboards for XP.

## Q3 2026: Intelligence & Personalization

- [ ] **AI Insights**: Personalized analysis of habits and mood.
- [ ] **Smart Reminders**: Adaptive notifications based on user behavior.
- [ ] **Content Hub**: Curated articles and videos for self-improvement.
- [ ] **Theme Store**: Marketplace for custom themes and icons.

## Q4 2026: Expansion

- [ ] **Web Version**: Full feature parity on the web.
- [ ] **Wearable App**: Companion app for Apple Watch and Wear OS.
- [ ] **Data Export**: Export user data to CSV/PDF.
- [ ] **Cloud Sync**: Optional cloud backup and sync across devices.

## Long-term Vision

- **Holistic Wellness Platform**: Integrating physical, mental, and emotional health tracking.
- **Coach Integration**: Connect with certified life coaches.
- **Enterprise Solutions**: Corporate wellness programs.
